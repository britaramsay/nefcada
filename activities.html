Dick Tibbits and Michael Hanrahan Honorees 2016

The late Dick Tibbits was a board member of NEFCADA, who maintained the longest tenure of any board member (20+years) in the history of the organization. This award is given to that individual who has demonstrated outstanding service as a member.


The Hanrahan award recognizes an individual's effort in the area of alcohol and drug abuse awareness, prevention, treatment and rehabilitation. The award is given in the name of Judge Michael E. Hanrahan to that individual who has demonstrated outstanding service in the community.
